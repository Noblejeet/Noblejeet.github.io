Supply Chain Planning aligns demand and supply to ensure a smooth, efficient flow of materials and information across the value chain. Below are key roles involved:

1. Demand Planner

  • Goal: Forecast customer demand to guide supply decisions
  • Key Tasks: Analyze sales data, collaborate with sales/marketing, build statistical forecasts
  • KPIs: Forecast Accuracy, Bias, Demand Variability

2. Supply Planner

  • Goal: Translate demand into executable supply plans
  • Key Tasks: Manage capacity, inventory targets, and production constraints
  • KPIs: Fill Rate, Inventory Turns, OTIF (On-Time In-Full)

3. Production Planner / Master Scheduler

  • Goal: Optimize factory schedules based on capacity and demand
  • Key Tasks: Create production schedules, prioritize orders, adjust for disruptions
  • KPIs: Schedule Adherence, Cycle Time, Throughput

4. Material Planner

  • Goal: Ensure timely availability of raw materials and components
  • Key Tasks: Monitor inventory, generate purchase orders, coordinate with procurement
  • KPIs: Material Availability, Shortage Rate, Inventory Accuracy

5. S&OP Analyst / IBP Coordinator

  • Goal: Facilitate end-to-end planning alignment across functions
  • Key Tasks: Run S&OP cycles, prepare scenarios, align supply plans with business strategy
  • KPIs: Consensus Forecast Accuracy, Plan Alignment, Inventory Optimization
